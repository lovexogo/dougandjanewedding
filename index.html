How we met
Sometime Feb, 2010

We were actually friends of friends on Facebook. No relationship was built throught the app but it let us meet!

The first day we met
Sometime Feb, 2016

Awkward, awkward, awkward...Long story short, Doug was there to meet a girl, I was there to meet a photographer because I was doing photoshoot at the time. I even brought my best friend with me just in case...

Started Building Relationship
28 May, 2010

Doug invited me to his BBQ party regardless of our not so great first meeting. And I showed up regardless too. Something sparked this time! Doug and I officialy decided that this is our first day of love. We have been celebrating our relationship on May 28th every year since!

Proposal
16 April, 2011

I sort of saw it coming but not the timing. Proposal took place at the Getty Villa when we were just having fun on the weekend. When Doug took out the ring, I was in shock! However, couple weeks before it happened, I was awake when Doug was measuring my finger in the middle of the night. :D

Signing the Papers: The Legal Part
03 June, 2011

We got legally married before our ceremony. We planned to have a backyard wedding when Doug's parents finish building their dream house in Aptos. And it's getting so close!
